About Mental Health

Mental health refers to our emotional, psychological, and social well-being, influencing how we think, feel, and interact with others. It plays a critical role in how we handle stress, build relationships, and make decisions, which is especially important for teenagers navigating academic, social, and personal challenges. Good mental health supports overall physical health, as unmanaged stress and emotional strain can lead to physical symptoms and illness. For young people, mental well-being is foundational for healthy development, academic success, and making positive life choices. Prioritizing mental health not only empowers individuals to reach their potential but also helps reduce the stigma around seeking support, creating a more understanding and resilient society.
